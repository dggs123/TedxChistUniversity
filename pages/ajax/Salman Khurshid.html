Salman Khurshid

Politician, Advocate

Salman Khurshid is a man known for his political presence in the country. He is an accomplished lawyer, an avid reader and has been an active member of the Indian National Congress for three decades. Having been a lecturer in law, his name is at the tip of every tongue in Trinity College, Oxford. His book, Beyond Terrorism: New Hope for Kashmir is renowned in the particular genre and has received rave reviews by critics. His play ‘Son of Babur’ also had a similar outcome. He is a man that takes up big responsibilities and follows up on them. He believes in the value of a choice and how it affects your course of action.
