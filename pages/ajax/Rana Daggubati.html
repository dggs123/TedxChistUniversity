Rana Daggubati

Actor

He is a famous Tollywood producer and actor, well known for his significant role as the lead antagonist in the movie, Bahubali. He has been a recipient of quite a few awards, not only as an actor but also as a visual effects supervisor. He comes from a family closely associated with the Telugu-Tamil movie industry, and did them proud when he won the Best Actor award for his very first film. Rana is an AR/VR enthusiast started out in 2005, working for FXLabs on video games, but gradually moved into the filmmaking industry. He has also walked the ramp several times. He is a multifaceted man, passionate about storytelling, and someone who is willing to experiment and take risks to accomplish his goals.
