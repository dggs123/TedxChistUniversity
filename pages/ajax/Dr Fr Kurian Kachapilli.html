Dr Kurian Kachappilly CMI

Professor Of Philosophy

Dr. Kurian Kachappilly is the professor of philosophy and religion at the DVK Bangalore, and a Visiting Faculty at the University of Bangalore (India), Christ University (Bangalore), K.U. Leuven (Belgium), FHWS (Germany), and Santo Tomas (the Philippines). He holds Master’s Degree in English Literature and Psychology, and a Licentiate and Doctorate in Philosophy from the K.U. Leuven, Belgium. Dr. Kachappilly is a prolific writer with several books and many scientific articles to his credit. His book ‘God of Love’ is well known the field of philosophy, both among his friends and critics. Besides over 25 years of teaching experience, he is a well-known organizer of International Conferences, like ‘Bounds of Ethics in a Globalized World’ (2014), in which His Holiness Dalai lama was the Chief Guest!
