Priti Salian

International Freelance Journalist

Priti S is a freelance writer and journalist who’s travelled to 18 diverse states of India, and across seven different countries of the world. She has written for international newspapers and media networks such as The Guardian, CNN Travel, Al Jazeera and The National among many others, and has covered a plethora of subjects from culture and travel to gender issues and human rights, from dining traditions to slum tourism. She has seen it all and she has documented it all. She may have picked up the profession by accident but everything happens for a reason and we’re sure quite a few people agree.
